About the Organizers

UPES-CSI (Computer Society of India) is a student-run organization at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in India. It is the oldest student chapter at UPES which aims to provide an environment to “Create, Share and Innovate” in the domain of Computer Science. For seven consecutive years we have been adjudged “The Best Student Chapter” across the country. The organization aims to promote the study of computer science as well as provide opportunities for networking, professional development, and community service. UPES-CSI organizes events such as workshops, seminars, and hackathons to help students gain practical skills and knowledge in the field of computer science.



About Hackathon 7.0


UPES-CSI has a legacy of organizing hackathons and this year we are launching our 7th edition, UPES CSI Hackathon 7.0, which is a multi specialization hackathon which will focus on the application of AI in domains of Blockchain, IoT, Cyber Security etc to develop intelligent solutions for society.

At UPES-CSI Hackathon 7.0, problem statements from various industrial and government sectors will be addressed. The outcomes of our Hackathon will solve problems from domains such as cybercrime, physically disabled people, natural disasters, ISRO and many more with AI-based solutions.

Our hackathon will also help students in gaining experience and taking part in interactive session's with industry experts, which enables students to clear up any questions and gain practical experience. Interactions with industry experts help students bridge the gap between their academic studies and cutting-edge technologies. The winners will be given internship opportunities at various industry partners' businesses and will be provided with cash prizes as well. Our hackathon will offer intelligent solutions for issues that exist in many different industries
